Organic additive library

Organic additives without guesswork.

Use compost, minerals, meals, biology, oils, soaps, and pest controls with a purpose. The rule is simple: match the additive to the problem, the plant, and the soil.

Do not stack amendments blindly.

For outdoor beds, test soil before repeated mineral or pH amendments. For containers, refresh old mix and feed lightly during active growth instead of trying to fix every symptom with one product.
