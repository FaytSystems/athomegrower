Plant diseases

The next visual library: fungal, bacterial, viral, and physiological problems with lookalikes and nonchemical prevention.

Must-have

Powdery mildew

White powdery patches; improve airflow, avoid crowding, remove infected leaves, and use labeled organic fungicides when appropriate.

Must-have

Rust diseases

Orange or brown pustules; remove infected foliage, avoid overhead watering, and select resistant varieties.

Must-have

Leaf spots

Brown, tan, black, or yellow-ringed spots; improve sanitation and reduce leaf wetness.

Should-have

Root rot

Wilting in wet soil; check roots, improve drainage, repot, and water less.

Should-have

Damping off

Seedlings collapse; use clean trays, airflow, and careful moisture.

Should-have

Viral symptoms

Mosaic, rings, distortion; isolate and discard confirmed infected plants.
