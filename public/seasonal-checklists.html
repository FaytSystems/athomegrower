Seasonal checklists

Monthly care reminders for indoor plants, patio growers, vegetable beds, and outdoor ornamentals.

Must-have

Spring

Repot, refresh soil, inspect pests, start seeds, prune winter damage.

Must-have

Summer

Water deeply, watch mites, shade heat-stressed containers, harvest herbs.

Must-have

Fall

Bring plants inside carefully, quarantine, reduce feeding, collect seeds.

Must-have

Winter

Reduce watering, increase light, watch fungus gnats, avoid cold drafts.
