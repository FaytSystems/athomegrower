Must-have problem finder

Symptom-first troubleshooting

For gardeners who know the symptom but not the cause. Start with what you see, then narrow it down.

Must-have

Yellow leaves

Could be watering, nitrogen, iron, magnesium, pests, low light, old leaves, or root stress.

Must-have

Brown tips

Often inconsistent watering, salts, low humidity, fluoride sensitivity, or potassium issues.

Must-have

Drooping

Can mean thirsty, overwatered, heat-stressed, cold-shocked, or root-damaged.

Must-have

Sticky leaves

Look for aphids, scale, mealybugs, whiteflies, and honeydew.

Should-have

Spots and patches

Compare pest stippling, fungal spots, sunburn, edema, and nutrient patterns.

Should-have

Curling leaves

Check water stress, aphids, mites, herbicide drift outdoors, heat, and humidity.
