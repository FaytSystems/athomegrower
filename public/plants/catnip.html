Plant care

Light:

Bright light is best. indoors, use a south window or supplemental grow light when stems stretch.

Soil mix:

Use a light container herb mix with compost, peat/coir, and perlite. avoid heavy garden soil in pots.

Soil moisture:

Evenly moist, not soggy.

When to water:

Water when the top half inch is dry. outdoor pots may need daily water in hot, sunny weather.

Fertilizer:

Feed lightly every couple of weeks indoors or as needed outdoors. avoid heavy feeding that weakens aroma and flavor.

Pruning:

Harvest often by pinching stem tips above leaf nodes to keep plants bushy.

Repotting/rootbound:

Repot when roots fill the pot or the plant dries out too quickly after watering.

Best use

Culinary, tea, fragrance, and pollinator herbs for containers, shelves, and garden beds.
