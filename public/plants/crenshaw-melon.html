Plant care

Light:

Full sun to bright light

Soil mix:

Rich, well-draining fruit or berry mix

Soil moisture:

Keep evenly moist during flowering and fruiting

When to water:

Keep evenly moist during flowering and fruiting

Fertilizer:

Feed lightly during active growth when the plant is producing new leaves, stems, flowers, or fruit.

Pruning:

Remove damaged growth and harvest or prune in small amounts so the plant can recover.

Repotting/rootbound:

Move container plants up one pot size when roots circle densely or watering becomes difficult.

Best use

Container fruit, edible landscapes, orchards, and sunny garden spaces
