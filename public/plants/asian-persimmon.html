Asian Persimmon

Diospyros kaki

Plant care

Light:

Full sun for best fruiting.

Soil mix:

Well-drained loam with steady moisture and good air movement.

Soil moisture:

Moderate and even moisture while fruit sizes.

When to water:

Water deeply during dry spells and avoid drought stress during fruit development.

Fertilizer:

Fertilize lightly; too much nitrogen can encourage fruit drop.

Pruning:

Keep pruning minimal, removing crossing, dead, or damaged wood.

Repotting/rootbound:

For patio trees, use large containers, refresh soil, root-prune when necessary, and avoid letting the root ball dry out.

Best use

Warm-climate edible landscapes and patios

Zones: usda zones 7-10

Problems to watch

Scale, psyllids, borers, leafrollers, and fruit drop are common things to watch.
