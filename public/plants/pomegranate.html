Pomegranate

Punica granatum

Plant care

Light:

Full sun and heat are best.

Soil mix:

Well-drained soil; tolerates leaner soils once established.

Soil moisture:

Moderate moisture; avoid wet roots.

When to water:

Water deeply but infrequently once established; containers need closer monitoring.

Fertilizer:

Use light spring feeding if growth is weak; avoid overfertilizing.

Pruning:

Train as a shrub or small tree and remove suckers, dead wood, and crowded growth.

Repotting/rootbound:

For patio trees, use large containers, refresh soil, root-prune when necessary, and avoid letting the root ball dry out.

Best use

Sunny patios

Zones: usda zones 7-10

Problems to watch

Fruit split from water swings, leaf spots, aphids, scale, and root stress.
