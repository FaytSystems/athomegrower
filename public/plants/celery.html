Apium graveolens

Plant care

Light:

Full sun to part sun; leafy crops can handle slightly less direct sun than fruiting crops.

Soil mix:

Use fertile, loose, well-drained soil with compost for steady growth.

Soil moisture:

Evenly moist.

When to water:

Water before the root zone dries hard. mulch outdoor beds to reduce moisture swings.

Fertilizer:

Use balanced fertility and avoid letting nitrogen run out during active leaf production.

Pruning:

Harvest outer leaves or thin crowded seedlings to improve airflow and reduce disease pressure.

Repotting/rootbound:

Use shallow to medium containers for greens; root crops need loose depth for straight roots.

Best use

Moist containers

Zones: usda zones 2-10

Flea beetles, aphids, caterpillars, damping off, downy mildew, and leaf spots.
