Plant care

Light:

Full sun to bright light

Soil mix:

Well-draining herb or vegetable potting mix

Soil moisture:

Keep evenly moist, then let the surface dry lightly

When to water:

Keep evenly moist, then let the surface dry lightly

Fertilizer:

Feed lightly during active growth when the plant is producing new leaves, stems, flowers, or fruit.

Pruning:

Remove damaged growth and harvest or prune in small amounts so the plant can recover.

Repotting/rootbound:

Move container plants up one pot size when roots circle densely or watering becomes difficult.

Best use

Kitchen gardens, containers, raised beds, and pollinator plantings

Problems to watch

Watch for aphids, mites, thrips, whiteflies, leaf spot, powdery mildew, damping off, and root rot.
