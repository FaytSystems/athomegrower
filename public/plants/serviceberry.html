Amelanchier spp.

Plant care

Light:

Full sun to part shade.

Soil mix:

Moist, well-drained soil; adaptable once established.

Soil moisture:

Even moisture during establishment and fruiting.

When to water:

Water young plants through dry periods; mulch to keep roots cool.

Fertilizer:

Usually light fertility only; avoid pushing weak growth.

Pruning:

Remove dead, crossing, or crowded stems after flowering or during dormancy.

Repotting/rootbound:

For containers, refresh mix and root-prune or up-pot when growth declines or roots fill the pot.

Best use

Edible landscapes, pollinator gardens, wildlife plantings

Zones: usda zones 4-8

Rust diseases, leaf spots, birds, and occasional insects.
