Plant care

Light:

Full sun to bright light

Soil mix:

Average to fertile, well-drained soil

Soil moisture:

Keep evenly moist while establishing

When to water:

Keep evenly moist while establishing

Fertilizer:

Usually not needed unless soil is extremely depleted.

Pruning:

Cut, mow, chop, or tarp before the crop sets unwanted seed.

Repotting/rootbound:

Move container plants up one pot size when roots circle densely or watering becomes difficult.

Best use

Soil building, erosion control, mulch, nitrogen capture, and garden rotations

Season: seasonal rotation crop

Planting window: Sow in the window that matches your climate, frost timing, and whether the cover is warm-season or cool-season.

Termination: Terminate before seed set unless reseeding is desired.

Uses: Soil cover, biomass, nutrient cycling, weed suppression, erosion control, pollinator support, or nitrogen fixation depending on the species.

Watch for aphids, mites, thrips, whiteflies, leaf spot, powdery mildew, damping off, and root rot.
