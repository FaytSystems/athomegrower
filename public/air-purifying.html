Air-purifying plant hub

Air-purifying plants, honestly framed.

These plants are commonly associated with indoor-air conversations, but real homes also need ventilation, cleaning, and humidity control. Choose them first because they are beautiful and manageable.

Featured plant cards

Each plant card has a real matched photo, ease score, soil type, watering trigger, light guidance, and practical notes.

Real plant photo
Must-haveVery easy

Snake plant

Light: Low to bright indirect light

Soil: Cactus/succulent mix or airy potting soil

Water: Water when soil is fully dry; usually every 2–4 weeks indoors.

Good first plant; tolerates neglect.

Real plant photo
Must-haveVery easy

Pothos

Light: Low to bright indirect light

Soil: All-purpose mix with perlite

Water: Water when top 1–2 inches are dry.

Fast trailing vine for shelves and offices.

Real plant photo
Must-haveEasy

Spider plant

Light: Bright indirect light

Soil: Well-draining potting mix

Water: Water when top inch dries.

Classic plant that produces pups.

Peace lily

Elegant foliage and white blooms.
