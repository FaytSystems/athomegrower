Editorial policy.

AtHomeGrower uses an organic-first, evidence-first, photo-first structure. Diagnosis pages explain uncertainty and encourage soil, pH, root, watering, and pest checks before treatment.

Advice hierarchy

  • University extension and botanical institutions first.
  • Peer-reviewed and government references where useful.
  • Experienced grower notes clearly separated from diagnosis claims.
  • Affiliate/product advice must never override plant health advice.

Diagnosis promise

We do not treat one photo as proof. We present likely causes, lookalikes, organic options, and when to test soil or contact local extension.
